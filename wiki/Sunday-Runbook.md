# Sunday Runbook

1. Open the app before the meeting starts.
2. Put the helper panel on the laptop and the display on the TV.
3. Pick the right mode: Speaker, Information, Song, or Prayer.
4. Start browser transcription if it is available.
5. Switch to OpenAI transcription if needed.
6. Use manual lines for anything urgent.
7. Tighten or loosen the display with `Margins`.
8. Increase or decrease `Text size` for the room.
9. Slow down or speed up `Update interval` if summaries are too fast or too slow.
